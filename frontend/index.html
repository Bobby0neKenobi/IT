About Space Invaders

Space Invaders is a classic arcade game that took the world by storm in 1978. Developed by Tomohiro Nishikado of Taito Corporation in Japan, it's widely considered to be one of the most influential video games of all time. Inspired by films like "War of the Worlds" and "The Andromeda Strain," Nishikado aimed to create a game with a sense of tension and achievement. He combined elements of target shooting games with the ability to destroy targets one by one, similar to the game Breakout.

Controls

Use 'A' to go left. Use 'D' to go right. Use space to shoot. When you hit an enemy you get 100 points. The big red orbs are explosives, which destroy the enemies in big quantaties, but you resive only 50 points for each destroyed invador. The small yellow orbs are machine gun power up it lasts for some time after that it stops!
